Project: Adjustable Compliant Nut CrackerDesign Philosophy: Adaptive CompressionThe design is based on a Compliant Compression Mechanism where two slender, flexible arms are connected by a curved compliant arch.Monolithic Motion: The tool operates entirely through elastic deformation. When the handle arms are pressed together, the arch deforms to generate controlled compressive force; once released, the stored elastic energy returns the tool to its open position.Hinge-Free Design: By eliminating pins, hinges, or sliding contacts, the design removes the need for assembly of discrete parts and prevents mechanical wear over repeated use.Simplified Assembly: The final product is a two-part PLA print that utilizes dovetail joints, completely removing the need for external fasteners.DfAM Innovations: Size-Adaptive GeometryA key feature of this project is its ability to handle different nut sizes without manual adjustments or additional components.Three-Level Jaw System: The inner jaw faces feature three progressively deeper recessed notches for small (almond), medium (hazelnut), and large (walnut) nuts.Passive Size Stops: These notches act as passive stops, automatically limiting jaw closure depth to prevent the edible kernel from being pulverized.Structural Optimization: The top arch was thickened and profiled to serve as the primary structural load path during the cracking process.Fabrication & Technical SpecificationsThe prototype was printed using Polylactic Acid (PLA) with specific settings to maximize durability:Print Orientation: The part was oriented vertically on the print bed to ensure layer lines run along the length of the thin compliant arch, maximizing inter-layer bond strength in high-stress zones.Infill & Walls: The arms feature a 4 mm wall thickness with a 25% gyroid infill, balancing the stiffness required to transmit cracking force against the flexibility needed for elastic return.Ergonomics: The handles are flared and tapered to allow for a natural palm squeeze, with integrated circular holes for reduced weight and hanging storage.Functional Testing ResultsTesting validated the concept’s ability to provide a controlled, progressive squeeze:Nut TypeTesting ObservationResultAlmond (~10 mm)Seated in the small slot;
