# ⚙️ CAD-MECHANICAL Portfolio

A curated collection of **mechanical CAD design projects** showcasing skills in 3D modeling, mechanical assemblies, compliant mechanisms, and industrial design — created by **Kunal Kongari**.

---

## 📁 Projects

### 🔩 Adjustable Compliant Nut Cracker
A compliant mechanism-based nut cracker designed with adjustability in mind. Leverages flexible members to transmit force without traditional rigid joints, reducing part count and improving reliability.

---

### ☕ Contemporary Slatted Coffee Table
A furniture design project featuring a modern aesthetic with slatted surfaces. Demonstrates proficiency in consumer product design, ergonomic proportioning, and woodworking-oriented CAD modeling.

---

### 🏭 Dual-Shaft Industrial Shredder
An industrial-grade dual-shaft shredder assembly. Highlights expertise in heavy machinery design, gear and shaft systems, and tolerance-aware mechanical assemblies suitable for manufacturing.

---

### 🏠 Modular Arch-Roof Shed System
A parametric, modular shed system with an arched roof profile. Focuses on scalable structural design, modular assembly planning, and construction-ready detailing.

---

### 🤖 Multi-Material Universal Compliant Gripper
A universal robotic gripper utilizing compliant mechanism principles and multi-material design strategies. Suitable for pick-and-place robotics applications requiring adaptability across object shapes.

---

### 🔧 OS&Y Globe Valve Design & Assembly
A complete design and assembly of an Outside Screw & Yoke (OS&Y) Globe Valve — a standard in industrial pipeline systems. Includes detailed part modeling and full assembly with realistic constraints.

---

### 🪜 Retractable Scissor Staircase Mechanism
A space-saving retractable staircase built on scissor-linkage kinematics. Demonstrates understanding of linkage mechanisms, motion simulation, and compact structural design.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills

- **CAD Software:** SolidWorks / Fusion 360 (or equivalent)
- **Design Domains:** Compliant mechanisms, industrial assemblies, furniture design, structural systems, robotics
- **Deliverables:** 3D models, assemblies, technical drawings
