Project: Multi-Material Universal Compliant GripperDesign Philosophy: Adapt for Additive Manufacturing (AfAM)The core of this project is the transformation of the Non-Inverting Lever Compliant Mechanism—originally designed for miniaturized triggering in devices like dot-matrix printers—into a versatile handheld tool.Mechanism Selection: The design utilizes a non-inverting topology where the input handle and output jaw move in the same direction. This counter-intuitive motion is achieved through the elastic deformation of flexure joints rather than traditional rigid pins or bearings.Monolithic-Style Assembly: While composed of six parts, the assembly uses dovetail joints to snap together by hand, completely eliminating the need for external fasteners, screws, or adhesives.Material Innovation: Locally Tuned StiffnessA key highlight of this 3D-printed project is the strategic use of multi-material FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) to dictate mechanical behavior.Rigid Framework (PLA): The frame and jaw bodies are printed in Polylactic Acid (PLA) to provide the necessary structural stiffness to hold heavy objects, such as a 150g padlock.Compliant Joints (TPU): Three hourglass-shaped flexure joints are printed in Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU). These flexible components provide the elastic deformation required for motion and act as a "passive return spring" to hold the jaws open at rest.Weight Optimization: To reduce material consumption while maintaining strength, the handle bodies feature an internal mesh-like hollow structure.Functional Capabilities & TestingThe gripper was fabricated at the M2D2 Lab at IISc Bengaluru and tested against objects of varying geometries and weights to validate its "universal" design:Object TestedWeight / GeometryResult2-Rupee Coin~5g / Thin flat discHeld stably via serrated jaw teeth and pre-load force.Padlock~150g / CylindricalAccommodated by rounded jaw profiles; held suspended in air.Tape Roll~40g / ToroidalSymmetric grip force centered the roll without deforming it.Electrical Charger~100g / RectangularSerrated surfaces prevented slipping even when tilted.Key Technical AdvantagesZero Backlash: Because there are no sliding parts or loose pins, the mechanism has no mechanical play.Tool-Free Assembly: The dovetail joint design allows for rapid manual assembly.Surface-Friendly: The compliant nature of the TPU and rounded jaw profiles allow for gripping delicate objects without scratching them.
